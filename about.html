About Scylox Blog

Your trusted source for entertainment news, reviews, and analysis across movies, TV shows, games, tech, and comics.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Abdelrahman Khaled, Scylox Blog began as a passion project to share insights about the entertainment industry. What started as a small blog has grown into a trusted resource for thousands of readers worldwide.

Our team consists of many dedicated writers and industry experts like Wilson Fisk who is passionate about delivering honest, in-depth reviews and the latest news across all forms of entertainment media.

We pride ourselves on maintaining editorial independence and providing unbiased opinions to help our readers make informed decisions about what to watch, play, and read.
